About The Developer

Hey, I'm Jacob Miller, an aspiring website designer and mobile app developer. I enjoy programming in my free time, especially making low poly video games. I also enjoy illustrating and making concept art in addition to my interest in weightlifting, running, and general fitness.

At the age of 12 I was introduced to Roblox and fell in love with coding through it. I would go on to create several low quality albeit popular games and win a couple of contests on the platform. I was comissioned by Roblox staff to help develop their Four Towers Bridge Battle in 2009.

Unfortunately in 2015 my account was lost when my email was compromised.

Theme Park Project

Theme Park Project began as a compilation of all the Roblox project files I still had.

Inspiration from Nintendo games along with my fascination of roller coasters would lead to the conception of Theme Park Project in 2016.

I had no real intention of releasing it and it become more of a simple hobby project to better my coding skills until in 2021 I decided to finish and release it so I could focus on new projects.

Project Theme Park is expected to release summer of 2023.

About

Theme Park Project is an online open-world social
hangout centered around a story taking place in a
fictional theme park. Featuring a cast of memorable
characters along with a collection of epic rollers
coasters and rides for players to experience.


Features
- 3 Chapter story-driven adventure
- Unlockable zones and rides
- Player titles and badges
- Customizable player owned housing
- Job system
- Competitive ride score system
- Dynamic NPCs

Game Info
- Platform: PC, Tablet, Mobile
- Genre: Low-Poly, Story, Social Hangout

- Release: Coming Summer 2023
